Why we need UI/UX Designers:

UI/UX Design Flaws
Unintuitive Navigation Menus that are hidden or labeled in ways that confuse users (like calling a logout button "exit the journey")
Overwhelming Choices Presenting too many options at once without organizing them
Inconsistent Design Using different styles, fonts, or button placements across pages—leaving users lost and frustrated
No Feedback for Actions Buttons or actions that don’t give any feedback, leaving users wondering if their input was registered
